## If Approval is Delayed

### Check Paddle Dashboard:
1. Go to Paddle Dashboard → Settings → Developer Tools
2. Check "Domain approval" section
3. Look for any messages or requirements

### Common Issues:
- **Pages not accessible:** Make sure your site is deployed and pages are live
- **Missing content:** Ensure all three pages have substantial content
- **Broken links:** Verify footer links work correctly

### Contact Paddle Support:
