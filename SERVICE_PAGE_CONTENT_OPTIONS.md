## Content Tips

1. **Be Specific:** Use concrete examples, not vague promises
2. **Show, Don't Tell:** Use visuals, case studies, examples
3. **Address Objections:** FAQ section should answer concerns
4. **Clear CTAs:** Multiple opportunities to contact/convert
5. **Mobile-Friendly:** All content should work on mobile
6. **SEO Optimized:** Include relevant keywords naturally
7. **Fast Loading:** Optimize images and content
8. **Accessible:** Follow WCAG guidelines
